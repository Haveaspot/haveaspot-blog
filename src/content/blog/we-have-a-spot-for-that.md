### **Introducing haveaspot**

Have a Spot is a new company that specializes in providing a curated list of community spaces available for hire in the UK. We understand that finding the perfect venue for an event or gathering can be time-consuming and stressful, which is why we have created an online platform that simplifies the process of venue booking. Our aim is to make it easier for users to find and book the perfect space for their event, while also helping venue managers to efficiently manage their bookings and payments.

### **We Have A Problem We Will Solve**

The UK is home to a vast range of community spaces available for hire, including village halls, scout halls, public spaces, and more. However, finding the perfect space for an event can be challenging due to the lack of centralized information and resources. Many venues are not widely advertised, and even those that are can be difficult to book and manage. This leaves many users frustrated and discouraged when searching for a suitable venue for their event.

### **We Have A Solution**

Have a Spot offers an online platform that provides a curated list of community spaces available for hire across the UK. Our platform simplifies the process of venue booking, allowing users to easily search for venues based on location, price, facilities, and availability. The platform also offers venue managers the ability to manage their bookings, show availability, list features such as disabled access, kitchens, toilets, and facilities, and take automated online bookings and payments. The Have a Spot app ensures that users can book their chosen venue in a few clicks, making it simple and stress-free.

### **We Have A Set Of Unique Features**

Our platform offers several unique features that set us apart from other venue booking services. Firstly, our database is carefully curated to include a wide range of community spaces available for hire, including those that are not widely advertised. This means that users have access to a greater selection of venues, giving them more options to choose from. Secondly, our platform allows for seamless booking and payment processes, ensuring that users can quickly and easily secure their chosen venue without any hassle. Thirdly, we provide venue managers with a suite of management tools that allow them to efficiently manage their bookings, showcase their venues, and take payments online, saving them time and effort.

### **We Have A Spot For Spots**

Have a Spot is the ultimate solution for anyone looking to book a community space in the UK. Our platform makes the process of finding and booking a venue simple and stress-free, while also providing venue managers with an efficient way to manage their bookings and payments. With our extensive database of community spaces, users have access to a greater range of venues, giving them more options to choose from. Whether you’re planning a wedding, a birthday party, hosting a class or holding a quiz night or just want somewhere convenient for your business meeting, Have a Spot has the perfect spot for you.
